Fate Stay Night: Unlimited Blade Work

The story revolves around Shirou Emiya, a hardworking and honest teenager who unwillingly enters a to-the-death battle royale called the Fifth Holy Grail War, where combatants fight with magic and Heroes throughout history for a chance to have their wishes granted. Orphaned and the sole survivor of a massive fire in Fuyuki City as a child, Shirou was taken in by a retired magus named Kiritsugu Emiya, who died some years later. Shirou's feelings of responsibility for those who died and his own salvation through his father formed a strong desire for justice and peace in him.
